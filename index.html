Hair Loss Treatment

Hair loss treatment refers to various methods and therapies designed to stop or reduce hair thinning and help promote hair regrowth. These treatments address different causes of hair loss, such as genetics, hormonal changes, stress, or poor nutrition.

Laser hair reduction

Laser hair reduction is a cosmetic procedure that uses focused light (laser) to target and damage hair follicles, inhibiting future hair growth. It provides a long-term solution for reducing unwanted body or facial hair and is known for being precise.

Acne (Pimples) Treatment

Acne treatment involves various methods to reduce pimples, blackheads, and blemishes caused by clogged pores, excess oil, or bacteria. Common treatments include topical creams (like benzoyl peroxide or salicylic acid), oral medications

hydra facial

A HydraFacial is a non-invasive facial treatment that uses a specialized machine to cleanse, exfoliate, extract impurities, and hydrate the skin. It typically includes the application of serums to improve skin tone, texture,.

Vitiligo Surgery

Vitiligo surgery involves procedures like skin grafting or tattooing to restore pigment to areas of skin affected by vitiligo, a condition that causes loss of skin color.

PRP/GFC

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) and GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) are treatments that use a patient's own blood to promote healing and regeneration. PRP involves extracting plasma rich in platelets, while GFC uses a higher concentration of growth factors.

Virginity surgery

Virginity surgery is a surgical procedure to reconstruct the hymen, often for cultural, religious, or personal reasons. It involves stitching the hymen to restore its appearance, though it doesn't restore virginity in a biological sense.

Hymenoplasty surgery

Hymenoplasty is a surgical procedure to reconstruct or repair the hymen, often for cultural, personal, or religious reasons. It involves stitching the hymen to restore its appearance.

CO2 fractional laser

CO2 fractional laser is a skin treatment that uses carbon dioxide laser beams to remove damaged skin layers, promoting skin renewal and improving texture, tone, and scars. It's commonly used for skin resurfacing and anti-aging.

Botox

Botox is a cosmetic treatment that involves injecting botulinum toxin to temporarily reduce wrinkles and fine lines by relaxing facial muscles. It is also used for medical purposes like treating migraines and excessive sweating.

Micro bledding / Permanent eybrow shaping

Microblading is a semi-permanent eyebrow shaping technique that uses tiny, hair-like strokes to apply pigment, creating natural-looking, fuller eyebrows. It lasts for 1-3 years, depending on skin type and care.

Skin lightning Treatment

Skin lightening treatment refers to procedures or products that reduce the appearance of dark spots, pigmentation, or uneven skin tone, often using ingredients like hydroquinone, vitamin C, or lasers to brighten the skin.
